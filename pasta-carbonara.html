Pasta Carbonara

A rich and creamy Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. Simple yet indulgent!

Ready In:

30 minutes

Serves:

4

Runtime:

30 minutes

Ingredients


400g spaghetti

100g pancetta (diced)

2 large eggs

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese

1 tsp black pepper (freshly ground)

1 tbsp olive oil

Salt to taste

Directions:

Step 1:


Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain.

Step 2:


In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat.

Step 3:


In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan, Pecorino Romano, and black pepper.

Slowly add reserved pasta water (a little at a time) to loosen the sauce.

Step 4:


Toss the hot pasta with the pancetta and remove from heat. 

Slowly mix in the egg-cheese mixture, stirring continuously to create a creamy sauce.

Adjust seasoning and serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.
