Garlic Bread

A crispy and flavorful side dish made with fresh garlic, butter, and herbs, perfect for any meal!

Ready In:

20 minutes

Serves:

4

Runtime:

20 minutes

Ingredients


1 baguette or Italian bread

1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)

4 cloves garlic (minced)

2 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped)

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:

Step 1:


Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2:


In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper until well combined.

Step 3:


Slice the baguette in half lengthwise and spread the garlic butter mixture evenly on both halves.

Step 4:


Place the bread on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden and crispy.

Step 5:


(Optional) Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top and broil for an additional 1-2 minutes for extra crispiness.

Step 6:


Remove from the oven, slice into pieces, and serve warm.
