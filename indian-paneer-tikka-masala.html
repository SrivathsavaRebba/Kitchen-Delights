Paneer Tikka Masala

Smoky, marinated paneer cubes simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based curry. A delightful vegetarian classic!

Ready In:

1 hour

Serves:

4

Runtime:

1 hour

Ingredients


250g paneer (cubed)

1/2 cup yogurt

1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

1 tsp red chili powder

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp garam masala

1 tsp cumin powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tbsp lemon juice

1 tbsp oil

1 onion (finely chopped)

2 tomatoes (pureed)

1/2 cup heavy cream

1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

1/2 tsp sugar

1 tbsp butter

Salt to taste

Fresh coriander for garnish

Directions:

Step 1:


In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, lemon juice, and salt.

Add paneer cubes, coat well, and let it marinate for 30 minutes.

Step 2:


Heat a pan, add oil, and cook the marinated paneer cubes until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

Step 3:


In the same pan, add butter and sauté onions until soft.

Add tomato puree, kasuri methi, sugar, and salt. Cook until oil separates.

Pour in heavy cream, mix well, and simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 4:


Add the cooked paneer to the sauce, mix gently, and cook for 5 minutes.

Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
