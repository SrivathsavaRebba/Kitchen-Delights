Hakka Noodles

A popular Indo-Chinese dish made with stir-fried noodles, fresh vegetables, and flavorful sauces!

Ready In:

30 minutes

Serves:

2-3

Runtime:

30 minutes

Ingredients


200g Hakka noodles

1 tbsp oil

1/2 cup cabbage (shredded)

1/2 cup carrot (julienned)

1/2 cup capsicum (thinly sliced)

1/2 cup spring onions (chopped)

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tsp vinegar

1 tsp chili sauce

1/2 tsp black pepper

Salt to taste

1 tsp garlic (minced)

Directions:

Step 1:


Boil water in a large pot. Add the Hakka noodles and cook as per the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.

Step 2:


Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add minced garlic and sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

Step 3:


Add cabbage, carrot, and capsicum. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender but still crunchy.

Step 4:


Add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, black pepper, and salt. Toss everything well to combine.

Step 5:


Stir-fry for another 2 minutes, ensuring the noodles are well coated with the sauces.

Step 6:


Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
