Burritos

A hearty Mexican dish made with a flour tortilla filled with a savory mixture of beans, meat, rice, cheese, and various toppings.

Ready In:

40 minutes

Serves:

2-3

Runtime:

40 minutes

Ingredients


For the Filling:

1 cup cooked rice

1/2 cup cooked black beans

1/2 cup cooked shredded chicken or beef

1/2 cup chopped bell peppers

1/4 cup chopped onions

1 tsp garlic (minced)

1 tbsp oil

1/2 tsp cumin powder

1/2 tsp chili powder

Salt to taste

For Assembly:

2 large flour tortillas

1/2 cup shredded cheese

1/4 cup sour cream

1/4 cup salsa

1/4 cup guacamole

Directions:

Step 1:


Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onions until soft.

Step 2:


Add chopped bell peppers and cook for 2 minutes.

Step 3:


Add cooked chicken or beef, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt. Stir well.

Step 4:


Add black beans and cooked rice. Mix thoroughly and cook for 2 more minutes.

Step 5:


Warm the tortillas on a pan for a few seconds on each side.

Step 6:


Place the filling in the center of each tortilla. Top with cheese, sour cream, salsa, and guacamole.

Step 7:


Fold in the sides and roll the tortilla tightly to form a burrito.

Step 8:


Serve warm and enjoy!
