Tacos

A delicious Mexican dish consisting of a crispy or soft tortilla filled with seasoned meat, fresh vegetables, and flavorful toppings.

Ready In:

30 minutes

Serves:

2-3

Runtime:

30 minutes

Ingredients


For the Filling:

1/2 lb ground beef or chicken

1/2 cup chopped onions

1 tsp minced garlic

1/2 tsp cumin powder

1/2 tsp chili powder

1/2 tsp paprika

Salt to taste

1 tbsp oil

For Assembly:

6 small corn or flour tortillas

1/2 cup shredded lettuce

1/2 cup diced tomatoes

1/4 cup shredded cheese

1/4 cup sour cream

1/4 cup salsa

1/4 cup guacamole

Directions:

Step 1:


Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.

Step 2:


Add ground beef or chicken and cook until browned.

Step 3:


Stir in cumin, chili powder, paprika, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 4:


Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.

Step 5:


Spoon the filling onto each tortilla.

Step 6:


Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, salsa, and guacamole.

Step 7:


Serve immediately and enjoy your tacos!
