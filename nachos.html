Nachos

A popular Mexican snack consisting of crispy tortilla chips topped with melted cheese, jalapeños, salsa, and other delicious toppings.

Ready In:

20 minutes

Serves:

2-3

Runtime:

20 minutes

Ingredients


Tortilla chips - 2 cups

Shredded cheddar cheese - 1 cup

Sliced jalapeños - 1/4 cup

Diced tomatoes - 1/2 cup

Sour cream - 1/4 cup

Salsa - 1/4 cup

Guacamole - 1/4 cup

Black beans (optional) - 1/2 cup

Chopped green onions - 1/4 cup

Directions:

Step 1:


Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2:


Arrange the tortilla chips in a single layer on a baking sheet.

Step 3:


Sprinkle shredded cheese evenly over the chips.

Step 4:


Add sliced jalapeños and black beans if using.

Step 5:


Bake in the oven for about 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 6:


Remove from oven and top with diced tomatoes, green onions, salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

Step 7:


Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious nachos!
