Pizza Margherita

Classic Italian pizza with a crispy crust, fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil. A timeless favorite!

Ready In:

1 hour

2-3 servings

Ingredients

For the Dough:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup warm water
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp dry yeast
1 tbsp olive oil

For the Toppings:
1/2 cup tomato sauce
200g fresh mozzarella (sliced)
Fresh basil leaves
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt to taste

Directions:

Step 1:

In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes until foamy.
Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into a soft dough.
Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.

Step 2:

Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
Roll out the dough into a thin circle and place on a pizza tray.
Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.

Step 3:

Add mozzarella slices and fresh basil leaves.
Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle salt.
Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and crispy.

Step 4:

Remove from oven, let it cool slightly, and slice.
Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Pizza Margherita!
